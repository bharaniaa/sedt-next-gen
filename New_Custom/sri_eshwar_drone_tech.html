About Us

Sri Eshwar Drone Tech Pvt Ltd is a premier drone technology and training organization dedicated to transforming industries and individuals with cutting-edge drone solutions and comprehensive training.

With a focus on innovation, safety, and client-centric solutions, we specialize in designing, developing, and deploying drones tailored for agriculture, infrastructure inspection, surveillance, mapping, environmental monitoring and more.

We are proud to be a certified Remote Pilot Training Organisation (RPTO), recognized by the Directorate General of Civil Aviation (DGCA).

Importance of Remote Pilot Certificate (RPC)

Why obtaining your RPC is essential for professional drone operations.

01

Legal Compliance

Legal compliance and adherence to regulations to avoid penalties and airspace violations.

02

Operational Safety

Operational and public safety through proper training and protocol adherence.

03

Restricted Airspace Access

Special permissions to access restricted airspace and expanded operational areas.

04

Industry Standards

Setting a benchmark with best practices for industry standards and professional recognition.

05

Enhanced Credibility

Enhances the credibility and professionalism of the drone industry in India.
