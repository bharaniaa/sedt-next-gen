About Us

About Us

Who we are ?

Sri Eshwar Drone Tech Pvt Ltd is a premier drone technology and training organization dedicated to transforming industries and individuals with cutting-edge drone solutions and comprehensive training. With a focus on innovation, safety, and client-centric solutions, we specialize in designing, developing, and deploying drones tailored for agriculture, infrastructure inspection, surveillance, mapping, environmental monitoring, and more. In addition to our technological offerings, we are proud to be a certified Remote Pilot Training Organization (RPTO), recognized by the Directorate General of Civil Aviation (DGCA). Our RPTO status allows us to provide Remote Pilot Certification programs designed to equip individuals with the skills, knowledge, and certifications required to safely and effectively operate drones. Our certification courses are tailored to meet industry standards, ensuring that trainees receive hands-on experience in drone operations, regulatory compliance, safety protocols, and airspace management.
